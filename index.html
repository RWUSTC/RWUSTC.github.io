Something about Me

Hefeng Xu (徐鹤峰) is an undergraduate student in University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). He was admitted in 2019 and is currently affiliated to Department of Modern Physics, School of Physical Science, majoring in theoretical physics.

His interest in research includes Pertubative Quantum Field Theory (pQFT), Standard Model, Topological Quantum Field Theory (TQFT), etc.

He recently works as a cadet in Interdisciplinary Center for Theoretical Study, USTC. His advisor is Prof. Yang Zhang
